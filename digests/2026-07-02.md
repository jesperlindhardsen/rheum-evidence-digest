## RA (Reumatoid artritis)

### RCT

**Long-term safety and efficacy of upadacitinib compared with adalimumab in patients with rheumatoid arthritis: 7-year data from the SELECT-COMPARE study** — RMD Open [T3], PMID 42342288. Take-home: 7-års opfølgning bekræfter vedvarende sikkerhed og effekt af upadacitinib sammenlignet med adalimumab ved RA.

### Guideline/konsensus

**2024 Update of the Asia-Pacific League of Associations for Rheumatology (APLAR) Recommendations for Rheumatoid Arthritis Management** — International Journal of Rheumatic Diseases [T4], PMID 42362943. Take-home: APLAR opdaterer sine RA-behandlingsanbefalinger for 2024.

**Healthcare providers' awareness and knowledge of recommendations on cardiovascular risk management in people with rheumatic and musculoskeletal diseases: a survey study** — EULAR Rheumatology Open [T4], PMID 42370102. Take-home: Kendskabet blandt behandlere til anbefalinger om kardiovaskulær risikostyring ved inflammatorisk reumatisk sygdom er mangelfuldt.

### Evidensopsummering

**Detectable safety gestalt of rheumatoid arthritis treatments from pivotal clinical drug trials** — Rheumatology (Oxford) [T3], PMID 42360790. Take-home: Samlet sikkerhedsmønster på tværs af pivotale RA-forsøg kan bruges til at nuancere klinisk risikovurdering af DMARDs.

**Effects of biologic and targeted synthetic disease modifying antirheumatic drugs (b/tsDMARDs) on patient-reported outcome domains in rheumatoid arthritis: a systematic review and network meta-analyses** — RMD Open [T3], PMID 42342282. Take-home: Netværksmetaanalyse rangerer b/tsDMARDs efter effekt på patientrapporterede outcomes ved RA.

**The Role of Therapeutic Apheresis in the Management of Rheumatoid Arthritis: A Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis** — Journal of Clinical Apheresis [T4], PMID 42389975. Take-home: Terapeutisk aferese kan overvejes som supplerende behandling ved svært behandlingsrefraktær RA, men evidensen er begrænset.

**Effectiveness of traditional Chinese medicine decoctions as an adjuvant treatment for rheumatoid arthritis: A systematic review and meta-analysis** — Medicine [T4], PMID 42363544. Take-home: Traditionel kinesisk medicin som tillægsbehandling viser mulig, men metodisk usikker effekt ved RA.

**Effectiveness of metformin as an adjunctive therapy in rheumatoid arthritis: a systematic review and meta-analysis of randomized controlled trials** — BMC Rheumatology [T4], PMID 42351297. Take-home: Metformin som tillægsbehandling ser lovende ud, men datagrundlaget er stadig spinkelt.

**Network meta-analysis of novel diagnostic biomarkers for rheumatoid arthritis: comparative performance of anti-CarP, anti-MCV, and emerging markers** — Frontiers in Immunology [T4], PMID 42382770. Take-home: Nye biomarkører som anti-CarP og anti-MCV kan supplere diagnostikken hos ACPA/RF-negative RA-patienter.

### Observationelle studier

**Trends in autoimmune rheumatic disease diagnoses before and after the COVID-19 pandemic in England: a population-based cohort study using OpenSAFELY** — The Lancet Rheumatology [T2], PMID 42379213. Take-home: Diagnosticeringsraterne for autoimmune reumatiske sygdomme faldt under pandemien og er endnu ikke fuldt normaliseret.

**Diagnostic and monitoring value of serum interleukin-33 and rs16924159 polymorphism in relation to disease activity and tumor necrosis factor inhibitor response in rheumatoid arthritis** — Journal of Rheumatic Diseases [T4], PMID 42388240. Take-home: Serum-IL-33 og en specifik polymorfi kan potentielt forudsige respons på TNF-hæmmere ved RA.

**Health education needs of the Brazilian population with rheumatoid arthritis: mixed-methods cross-sectional study** — Rheumatology International [T4], PMID 42384192. Take-home: RA-patienter efterspørger mere struktureret sygdomsspecifik patientuddannelse.

**Retention Rates of Biological Disease-Modifying Antirheumatic Drugs in Patients With Rheumatoid Arthritis and Reduced Kidney Function: Analysis From the IORRA Cohort** — International Journal of Rheumatic Diseases [T4], PMID 42362486. Take-home: Nedsat nyrefunktion påvirker valg og fastholdelse af biologisk behandling ved RA.

**Acthar Gel treatment in patients with rheumatoid arthritis, systemic lupus erythematosus, or dermatomyositis/polymyositis: analysis of physician-reported charts** — Journal of Comparative Effectiveness Research [T4], PMID 42347561. Take-home: Real-world data peger på klinisk anvendelighed af ACTH-gel som tillægsbehandling ved svært behandlelig RA, SLE og myositis.

**Rheumatoid factor production is genetically and molecularly distinct from rheumatoid arthritis** — The Journal of Clinical Investigation [T4], PMID 42378266. Take-home: RF-produktion har en selvstændig genetisk baggrund adskilt fra selve RA-sygdomsprocessen, hvilket kan nuancere forståelsen af serologisk subklassificering.
